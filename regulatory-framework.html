Tax Incentives & Investment Benefits

Tax and Customs Codes ‚Ä¢ Updated 2025

Up to 30-year tax holidays for renewable energy projects
  • Full exemption from corporate and personal income tax (up to 30 years)
  • 100% exemption from property and land tax
  • VAT and customs duty waived on imported RE equipment
  • Feed-in tariffs via guaranteed PPAs with state power companies
  • Investment Promotion Documents: 50% profit-tax cut for 7 years for strategic RE projects (Presidential Decree No.2462, Feb 2024)
  • Small-scale incentives: Residential solar up to 150 kW tax-exempt; rooftop PV up to 200 kW installable without permits
  • Revenue certainty: Long-term PPAs with Azerenergy/Azerishiq at predetermined prices
  • Equipment imports: Duty-free for panels, turbines, and all renewable energy equipment
  • 2025 Tax Code: Reinforced profit, property, land, and import-VAT exemptions for green PPP projects
