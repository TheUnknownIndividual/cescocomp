Data for Policy, Planning & Implementation

This platform addresses the competition's second theme by creating a comprehensive knowledge hub that:

  • Connects Stakeholders: Digital platform linking renewable energy startups, investors, and strategic partners with actionable data
  • Showcases Clean Energy Potential: National and regional visualization of Azerbaijan's 27 GW renewable capacity across solar, wind, and hydro
  • Provides Regulatory Clarity: Comprehensive database of laws, action plans, NDC commitments, and investment frameworks
  • Enables Strategic Planning: Rayon-level data for policymakers to identify priority zones and infrastructure gaps
  • Accelerates Implementation: Real-time tracking of operational projects, construction pipelines, and remaining potential
  • Promotes Youth Leadership: Open-access platform empowering young entrepreneurs and innovators with data-driven insights
