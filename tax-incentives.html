Tax Incentives & Investment Benefits for Renewable Energy

Updated 2025 ‚Ä¢ Republic of Azerbaijan

Azerbaijan provides a comprehensive range of tax and customs incentives to promote investment in the renewable energy sector. These incentives are designed to attract both local and international capital and support the nation's goal of generating 30% of its electricity from renewable sources by 2030.

"Strategic tax holidays and customs waivers reduce the cost of green transition and encourage private investment."

Key Tax Exemptions

Projects operating under Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs) or Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) benefit from substantial tax relief:

  • Corporate Income Tax Exemption: Profits from renewable energy production are exempt from income tax for up to 30 years.
  • Property Tax Exemption: Property utilized for renewable energy production is exempt from property tax for up to 30 years.
  • Land Tax Exemption: Land allocated for renewable energy projects is exempt from land tax for up to 30 years.

Customs & VAT Benefits

To reduce upfront capital costs, the government offers:

  • Import Duties Waiver: Machinery, technological equipment, and devices imported for renewable energy production are exempt from customs duties.
  • VAT Exemption: Imports of the above-mentioned equipment are also exempt from Value Added Tax (VAT) for up to 30 years.

Residential Incentives

Private residential consumers are also encouraged to adopt green energy:

  • Income Tax Exemption: Income generated from private electricity production using renewable sources (up to 150 kW capacity) is exempt from income tax.
